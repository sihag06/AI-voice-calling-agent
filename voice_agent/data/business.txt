Business Name: Walk In Beauty Thai Spa

Overview:
Walk In Beauty Thai Spa is a modern Thai wellness spa known for its Traditional Thai Massage therapies. 
The spa focuses on authentic techniques aimed at relieving stress and improving muscle flexibility.

Services:
The spa offers Traditional Thai Massage as its core service. 
Additional wellness services include aromatherapy massages, body scrubs, and facial treatments, which are often suggested based on customer needs.

Pricing:
A 60-minute Traditional Thai Massage session is typically priced between ₹1,200 and ₹1,500, depending on therapist availability and time of day.

Ambiance:
The spa features a modern and chic interior with soft lighting, contemporary décor, and private therapy rooms designed for comfort and relaxation.

Session Duration:
The standard session duration for Traditional Thai Massage is 60 minutes, with options to extend if required.

Availability:
The spa usually operates until late evening and may offer appointment slots around 10:00 PM, subject to availability.

Location:
Located on 80 Feet Road in Indiranagar, Bengaluru, the spa is easily accessible and popular among local residents.
